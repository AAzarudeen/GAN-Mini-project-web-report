Abstract

  This mini project proposes a novel idea using the GENERATIVE AI (GAN) in architectrue. The goal of this mini project is to generate a architectral design for a given plot area. In this project there are two GANs involved one is the GAN which will help us to clean the images i.e. the raw datas which were collected for the training of the plot generation GAN. The role of our team in this mini project was to clean the data i.e. the images of various plot designed by various architects to train the cleaning GAN. Raw images are collected using webscraping method those images are based on the North American and Europe region. Those images are to be cleaned the way to train the GAN model. As a part of this mini-project a Auto Encoder code was given with some intentional bugs for learning purpose.

Method

1-Problem Statement

  Plot designed by the architects are always a time consuming work for them and also most of the time based on the customer's constrainds it take lot of work and time. To reduce the plot designing time we are going to bulid a GAN AI model which can generate the Plots based on the user's or customer's prompt. The role given for us is to clean as many as images possible to train the GAN.

2-Data Collection

  In the initial stage we were given a data consisting images around 6000 which were download in bulk using webscraping method. Those 6000 images are splited among the 6 teams for our team we got around 1000 images and in those images many images were irrelavent and most of images were so blury and uneditable. The number of images which were good to edit (or) clean were around 400.
